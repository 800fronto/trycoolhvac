# Items Needed from Business Owner

Things we need from the CoolHVAC business owner to complete the SEO overhaul. We can build the site structure and placeholder content, but these items require real business information.

---

## Critical (Blocks Local Pack Ranking)

- [ ] **Physical business address** — Street address for the business (or confirmation that this is a service-area business with no storefront). Google's local algorithm heavily weights NAP consistency.
- [ ] **Contractor license numbers** — DC, MD, and VA contractor license IDs to display on the site. Verifiable credentials are a core trust signal.
- [ ] **NATE certification IDs** — Actual certification numbers for technicians to display alongside claims.
- [ ] **Google Business Profile access** — URL to existing GBP listing, or confirmation that one needs to be created. GBP is the single most important factor for Local Pack ranking.

## High Priority (Blocks Content Quality)

- [ ] **Real customer reviews** — At least 6-12 real testimonials with: full name (with permission), city, service type, date, and review text. Currently using placeholder reviews.
- [ ] **Aggregate review source** — Where does the "4.9/5 from 500+ reviews" claim come from? Link to Google Reviews, Yelp, or other platform so we can verify and link to it.
- [ ] **Social media profiles** — URLs for Facebook, Instagram, Google Business, Yelp, or any active profiles. Footer currently has dead social links.
- [ ] **Team photos** — Real photos of technicians, vans, and job sites. Currently using Unsplash stock photos.
- [ ] **Owner/founder bio** — Name, background, why they started the company, years in the industry. For the About page.
- [ ] **Insurance details** — Insurance provider and policy type (general liability, workers comp) for the trust section.

## Medium Priority (Enhances Authority)

- [ ] **Manufacturer partnerships** — Are you an authorized dealer for Carrier, Lennox, Trane, etc.? Which brands specifically? We list 8 brands but need to confirm which are actual partnerships vs. just serviced.
- [ ] **Industry memberships** — ACCA, BBB, local Chamber of Commerce, ENERGY STAR partner, etc.
- [ ] **Service area confirmation** — Are all 163 locations in `data/locations.js` accurate? Any to add or remove?
- [ ] **Pricing ranges** — Approximate cost ranges for common services (AC repair, furnace repair, installation, tune-up). The quote calculator uses placeholder numbers.
- [ ] **Financing partners** — Do you actually offer financing? Through whom? (Synchrony, GreenSky, etc.)
- [ ] **Custom domain** — Is `trycoolhvac.com` (or similar) registered? The vercel.app subdomain carries no domain authority. A custom domain is essential for SEO.

## Nice to Have (Long-term SEO)

- [ ] **Case studies** — 2-3 detailed project stories (problem, solution, outcome) with photos. Great for E-E-A-T signals.
- [ ] **Before/after photos** — From real jobs to demonstrate experience.
- [ ] **Video content** — Technician introductions, how-to tips, job walkthroughs. Video builds trust and dwell time.
- [ ] **Warranty details** — Specific warranty terms for labor and parts.
- [ ] **Emergency response metrics** — Average response time for emergency calls (e.g., "average 45-minute response time"). Concrete numbers beat vague claims.
